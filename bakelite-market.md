<p><p>The Bakelite market is categorized into three main types based on solubility: alcohol-soluble, modified, and oil-soluble Bakelite. Alcohol-soluble Bakelite is used in applications like adhesives and coatings requiring quick-drying properties. Modified Bakelite features enhanced formulations for improved performance in diverse industries such as automotive and electrical. Oil-soluble Bakelite, often utilized in lubricants and greases, offers excellent thermal stability and resistance to degradation. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to specific industrial needs and enhancing product functionality.</p></p>
