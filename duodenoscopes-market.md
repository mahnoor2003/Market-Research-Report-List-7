In contrast, electronic duodenoscopes integrate advanced camera technology and digital imaging, delivering higher resolution and enhanced visualization. Both types are used for diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in the duodenum, with electronic duodenoscopes gaining popularity due to improved image quality and user convenience.</p></p>
