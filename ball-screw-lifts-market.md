<p><p>Ball screw lifts are classified into several types: rotary ball screws, translation ball screws, and others. Rotary ball screws convert rotational motion into linear movement, commonly used in applications requiring high precision and efficiency. Translation ball screws, on the other hand, focus on linear motion directly, often employed in vertical lift systems. Other types include specialized configurations for unique applications, which cater to specific industry needs, enhancing versatility and performance in various mechanical and automation systems.</p></p>
