<p><p>Cleanroom pass-through boxes are essential for maintaining sterile environments in industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology. In contrast, the dynamic market includes pass-through boxes equipped with airflow systems, enabling a continuous flow of air to prevent contamination during material transfer. Both types serve specific cleanroom protocols, ensuring safety and compliance with hygiene standards.</p></p>
