<p><p>The aliphatic polyglycols market includes three main types: polyethylene glycol (PEG), polypropylene glycol (PPG), and polytetrahydrofuran (PTHF). PEG is widely used in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics as a solvent and humectant. PPG serves as an important polyol in the production of polyurethane foams and lubricants. PTHF, known for its elasticity and chemical resistance, is employed in spandex fibers and coatings. Each type has distinct properties that cater to various industrial applications, driving their market demand.</p></p>
