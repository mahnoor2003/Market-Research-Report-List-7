<p><p>The automotive parking aid system market is categorized into two main types: fully automatic/intelligent parking assistance systems and non-fully automatic parking assistance systems. Fully automatic systems enable vehicles to autonomously park without driver intervention, utilizing advanced sensors and artificial intelligence. In contrast, non-fully automatic systems assist drivers by providing visual and auditory cues while still requiring driver control to maneuver the vehicle into the parking space. These technologies aim to enhance parking efficiency and reduce the risks of accidents.</p></p>
