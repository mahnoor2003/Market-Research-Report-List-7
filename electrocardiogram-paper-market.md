<p><p>The electrocardiogram (ECG) paper market consists of various formats, primarily single roll and Z-fold types. Single roll ECG paper offers continuous printing, making it suitable for standard devices needing uninterrupted data output for heart activity monitoring. In contrast, Z-fold ECG paper features pre-folded sections, allowing for easier handling and storage after printing. This format is often preferred for longer monitoring sessions, ensuring efficient use of space while maintaining clear, readable results for analysis and patient records.</p></p>
