<p><ul><li>Standard Drag Chain Conveyors</li><li>En-masse Drag Chain Conveyors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drag chain conveyor market is divided into two main types: standard drag chain conveyors and en-masse drag chain conveyors. Standard drag chain conveyors are designed for transporting bulk materials along a predefined path using a series of chains with attachments. In contrast, en-masse drag chain conveyors move materials by pushing bulk product along a channel, maximizing the load capacity and reducing the amount of space required. Both types are widely used in various industries for efficient material handling.</p></p>
