<p><p>The plastic lumber market comprises three main types: virgin plastic, recycled plastic, and composite materials. Virgin plastic lumber is made from new, non-recycled plastic, ensuring high durability and aesthetic appeal, often used in high-end applications. Recycled plastic lumber uses post-consumer plastic waste, promoting sustainability and reducing landfill contributions. Composite lumber combines wood fibers with plastic, offering a natural appearance and resistance to decay, making it suitable for various outdoor applications. Each type addresses different consumer needs while enhancing environmental performance.</p></p>
