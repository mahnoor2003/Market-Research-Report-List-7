<p><p>The organic phthalocyanine pigment market is primarily divided into two types: metal phthalocyanine pigments and metal-free phthalocyanine pigments. Metal phthalocyanine pigments, which contain metals like copper or cobalt, are known for their vibrant colors and excellent lightfastness, making them popular in coatings, inks, and plastics.
