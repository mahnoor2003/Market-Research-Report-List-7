<p><ul><li>CBM (Conventional Buoy Mooring)</li><li>SPM (Single Point Mooring)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil offloading systems market encompasses various mooring technologies, primarily focusing on Conventional Buoy Mooring (CBM) and Single Point Mooring (SPM) systems. CBM utilizes fixed buoy structures for offloading oil from tankers to shore, offering steady operations in calm conditions. In contrast, SPM systems feature a buoy that allows tankers to pivot around a single anchor point, accommodating rough weather and larger vessels. These systems enhance operational efficiency, safety, and flexibility in offshore oil transfer processes.</p></p>
