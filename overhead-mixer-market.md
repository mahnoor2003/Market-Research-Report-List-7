<p><ul><li>Constant Rate</li><li>Controllable Rate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overhead mixer market can be categorized into two types: constant rate and controllable rate. In a constant rate market, the mixer operates at a fixed speed and power, providing consistent mixing results regardless of material variations. In contrast, a controllable rate market allows operators to adjust the speed and intensity of mixing based on specific processing requirements, enhancing flexibility and efficiency. Each type serves different industrial needs, depending on the complexity of the mixing application and material properties.</p></p>
