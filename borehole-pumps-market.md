<p><strong>The Borehole Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multistage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The borehole pumps market is primarily categorized into single-stage and multistage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for low to medium depth applications and are typically used for larger flow rates at lower pressure. In contrast, multistage pumps are engineered to handle deeper boreholes, generating higher pressure for applications requiring significant lift. These pumps are often employed in water supply, irrigation, and industrial processes, with multistage varieties favored for their efficiency in overcoming greater elevation differences.</p></p>
