<p><p>The automotive solar control window film market consists of three main types: clear, dyed, and vacuum coated films. Clear films are transparent, providing UV protection without altering vehicle aesthetics. Dyed films incorporate color pigments to reduce glare and enhance privacy while absorbing heat. Vacuum coated films utilize a metallic layer to reflect solar energy, offering superior heat rejection and durability. Each type serves different consumer preferences for comfort, privacy, and style while enhancing vehicle aesthetics and performance.</p></p>
