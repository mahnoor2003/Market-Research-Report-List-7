<p><strong>The Hydrophobic Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fumed</li><li>Precipitated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrophobic silica, used in coatings, adhesives, and composites, is categorized into fumed and precipitated types. Fumed hydrophobic silica is produced through high-temperature vapor-phase hydrolysis, offering high purity, low bulk density, and excellent thickening and reinforcing properties. Precipitated hydrophobic silica is made via wet chemical processes, providing cost-effectiveness, higher bulk density, and good dispersibility. Both types are surface-treated to repel water, enhancing compatibility with non-polar systems, but fumed silica is preferred for high-performance applications, while precipitated silica suits cost-sensitive industries.</p></p>
