<p><strong>The Automotive Battery Management Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BEV Battery Management Controller</li><li>PHEV Battery Management Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Battery Management Controller (BMC) market is divided primarily into two types: Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV) BMC and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) BMC. BEV BMCs manage and optimize energy storage systems in fully electric vehicles, ensuring safety, efficiency, and longevity of the battery. In contrast, PHEV BMCs cater to vehicles that utilize both electric and internal combustion engines, balancing energy flow between the battery and combustion systems. Both controllers play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance and reliability.</p></p>
