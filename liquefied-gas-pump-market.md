<p><strong>The Liquefied Gas Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Pumps</li><li>Rotatory Pumps</li><li>Diaphragm Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquefied gas pump market includes three main types: positive displacement pumps, rotary pumps, and diaphragm pumps. Positive displacement pumps work by trapping a fixed volume of gas and forcing it through the discharge pipe, ensuring consistent flow rates. Rotary pumps utilize rotating mechanisms to move gas, offering high efficiency and smooth flow. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create a vacuum and draw gas, allowing for precise control and handling of corrosive liquids. Each type serves different applications and operational needs.</p></p>
