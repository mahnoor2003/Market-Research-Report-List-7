<p><strong>The Solid Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cured On Solid Tire</li><li>Pressed On Solid Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid tire market comprises primarily two types: cured-on solid tires and pressed-on solid tires. Cured-on solid tires are manufactured through a curing process, where rubber compounds are vulcanized, providing durability and resistance to wear. They are ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, pressed-on solid tires are created by directly pressing a solid rubber compound onto a wheel, offering ease of installation and flexible design options. Both types cater to industries requiring reliable tire performance in challenging environments.</p></p>
