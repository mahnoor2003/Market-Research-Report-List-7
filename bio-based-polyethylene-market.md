<p><p>The bio-based polyethylene market includes three main types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). HDPE is known for its strength and rigidity, making it suitable for containers and pipes. LLDPE offers flexibility and stretchability, ideal for films and packaging. LDPE is characterized by its lightweight and low-density properties, used in easy-to-tear films and squeeze bottles. All three types are derived from renewable resources, contributing to sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
