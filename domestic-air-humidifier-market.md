<p><p>The domestic air humidifier market features three main types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Humidifiers** use high-frequency vibrations to produce a fine mist, making them energy-efficient and quiet.</p><p>2. **Hot Steam Type Humidifiers** boil water to create steam, effectively sanitizing the air but consuming more energy and producing heat.</p><p>3. **Pure Humidifiers** generally refer to natural or filter-based systems that add moisture without altering air temperature, focusing on health and environmental benefits. Each type caters to varying needs for humidity control in homes.</p></p>
