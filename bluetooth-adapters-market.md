<p><p>The Bluetooth adapters market is segmented primarily into single-band and dual-band categories. In contrast, dual-band Bluetooth adapters can function on both the 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing enhanced speed and reduced interference for high-performance applications.
