<p><p>The grease meter market is divided into two main types: mechanical meters and electronic meters. Mechanical meters operate through gears and physical movement to measure grease flow, offering simplicity and durability in various industrial applications. In contrast, electronic meters utilize sensors and digital displays for precise measurements, often incorporating advanced features like data logging and real-time monitoring. Both types cater to different user preferences, with mechanical meters favored for their reliability and electronic meters preferred for their accuracy and functionality.</p></p>
