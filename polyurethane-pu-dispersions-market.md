<p><p>The Polyurethane (PU) Dispersions market consists of two primary types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based PU dispersions are environmentally friendly, offering low volatility organic compounds and are widely used in coatings, adhesives, and textiles. They provide good adhesion, flexibility, and durability. In contrast, solvent-based PU dispersions contain organic solvents, delivering superior performance in harsh conditions but pose higher environmental and health risks. Both types cater to diverse applications across industries, driven by performance requirements and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
