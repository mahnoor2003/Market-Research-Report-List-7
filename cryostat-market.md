<p><ul><li>Closed-Cycle Cryostats</li><li>Continuous-Flow Cryostats</li><li>Bath Cryostats</li><li>Multistage Cryostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryostat market encompasses various types designed for specific cooling applications. Closed-cycle cryostats utilize a refrigeration cycle to achieve low temperatures without the need for external coolant, ensuring efficiency and convenience. Continuous-flow cryostats circulate a refrigerant for a steady cooling effect, ideal for ongoing experiments. Bath cryostats employ a liquid coolant to maintain temperature stability for samples submerged in fluid. Multistage cryostats utilize multiple cooling stages to reach extremely low temperatures, catering to advanced scientific research and applications.</p></p>
