<p><ul><li>Line-scan System</li><li>Full-frame System</li><li>Break-beam Timing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Track and Field Timing System market includes three main types: Line-scan systems, which utilize a moving camera to capture images of runners' finishes in real time; Full-frame systems that take high-resolution photographs at the finish line for precise timing; and Break-beam timing systems that rely on beams of light activated by athletes as they cross the finish line. Each system offers unique advantages in accuracy and reliability, catering to different event requirements in track and field competitions.</p></p>
