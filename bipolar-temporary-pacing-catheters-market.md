The market is divided into two main types: temporary pacing catheters without balloons and those with balloons. In contrast, catheters with balloons incorporate an inflatable mechanism to assist with positioning and stabilization, enhancing the effectiveness of pacing in more complex cardiac interventions.</p></p>
