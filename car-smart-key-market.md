<p><p>The car smart key market encompasses two main types: Passive Keyless Access (PKES) and Remote Keyless Access (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically using a key, as the system detects the key fob's presence nearby. Conversely, RKES requires users to press buttons on the key fob to unlock or start the vehicle. Both technologies enhance convenience and security, catering to consumer demand for modern features in automotive access systems.</p></p>
