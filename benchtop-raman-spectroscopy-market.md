<p><p>Benchtop Raman spectroscopy market includes two primary types: invasive and non-invasive Raman spectrometers. Invasive Raman spectrometers are designed to probe samples by direct contact, often used for detailed analysis of solids, liquids, and biological materials. In contrast, non-invasive Raman spectrometers allow for remote analysis without direct contact, making them ideal for applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and medical diagnostics. Both types play a vital role in enhancing analytical capabilities across various industries by providing molecular-level insights.</p></p>
