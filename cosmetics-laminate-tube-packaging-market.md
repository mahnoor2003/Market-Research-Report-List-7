<p><strong>The Cosmetics Laminate Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cosmetics laminate tube packaging typically comes in two main types: ABL ( Aluminium Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). </p><p>ABL tubes are made with a layer of aluminum, providing excellent protection against light, air, and moisture, making them ideal for sensitive products. In contrast, PBL tubes utilize layers of plastic materials, offering flexibility and lightweight benefits. While ABL is often preferred for premium products requiring extended shelf life, PBL is popular for its cost-effectiveness and suitability for various cosmetic items.</p></p>
