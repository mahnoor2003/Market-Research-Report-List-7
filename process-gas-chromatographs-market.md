<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The process gas chromatography market includes two main types: gas-solid chromatography (GSC) and gas-liquid chromatography (GLC). GSC separates components based on their adsorption to solid adsorbents, making it ideal for analyzing various gaseous compounds. In contrast, GLC utilizes a liquid stationary phase to separate and analyze volatile compounds in a gas phase, allowing for enhanced sensitivity and resolution. Both methods are essential for industrial applications, environmental monitoring, and quality control in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
