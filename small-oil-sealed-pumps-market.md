<p><ul><li>Rotary Vane Pump</li><li>Screw Pump</li><li>Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small oil sealed pumps market comprises various types, including rotary vane pumps, screw pumps, and piston pumps. Rotary vane pumps employ sliding vanes to create a vacuum for oil transfer, making them efficient for low-viscosity fluids. Screw pumps utilize revolving screws to convey fluids, offering continuous and smooth operation, ideal for higher viscosity oils. Piston pumps function by using reciprocating pistons to generate pressure, providing accurate flow rates and high pressures suitable for various industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs within the oil industry.</p></p>
