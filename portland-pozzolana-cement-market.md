<p><p>The Portland Pozzolana Cement market is divided into two main types: Artificial Pozzolana Cement and Natural Pozzolana Cement. Artificial Pozzolana Cement is produced by mixing Portland cement with pozzolanic materials, such as fly ash or slag, enhancing durability and decreasing permeability. In contrast, Natural Pozzolana Cement is derived from naturally occurring volcanic materials, offering unique properties that improve performance. Both types are valued for their eco-friendliness and ability to reduce the carbon footprint in construction applications.</p></p>
