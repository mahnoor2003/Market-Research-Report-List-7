<p><p>The multifunctional label adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives are environmentally friendly, featuring low volatile organic compound (VOC) content, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including food and beverage labeling. In contrast, solvent-based label adhesives offer stronger bonding properties and enhanced durability, making them ideal for challenging conditions and outdoor applications. Both types serve diverse industries, catering to varying performance requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
