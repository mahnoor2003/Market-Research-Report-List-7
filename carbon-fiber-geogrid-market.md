<p><p>The carbon fiber geogrid market primarily consists of two types: biaxial and uniaxial geogrids. Biaxial geogrids provide reinforcement in two directions, enhancing load distribution and stability, making them suitable for various applications like ground stabilization and paving. In contrast, uniaxial geogrids are designed for reinforcement in one direction, primarily used in retaining walls and slope stabilization. Both types leverage the lightweight yet strong properties of carbon fiber, making them effective for construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
