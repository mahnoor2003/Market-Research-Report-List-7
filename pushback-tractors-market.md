<p><p>The pushback tractors market consists of two primary types: towbarless tractors and conventional tractors. Towbarless tractors offer enhanced maneuverability, allowing for easy movement of aircraft without the need for traditional tow bars, making them ideal for modern airport operations. On the other hand, conventional tractors use a towbar system to connect to aircraft, often being more versatile for various ground handling tasks. Both types are essential for efficient aircraft handling, each serving distinct operational needs in the aviation industry.</p></p>
