<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Charger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-board Charger</li><li>Off-board Charger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric vehicle charger market is primarily divided into two types: on-board chargers and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated within the vehicle, converting AC power from an external source to DC for the battery. They are typically slower in charging speed and suitable for home or standard charging stations. Off-board chargers, often found in public charging stations, are external units that provide higher power levels, enabling faster charging times and enhancing the convenience for electric vehicle users.</p></p>
