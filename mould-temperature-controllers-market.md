<p><p>Mould temperature controllers are essential in industries like plastics and metalworking, ensuring precise temperature management during the production process. The market can be categorized into two main types: oil type and water type. Oil type controllers utilize thermal oil to provide consistent heating, offering higher temperature capabilities and stability, ideal for larger molds. Water type controllers use water as a coolant, providing rapid temperature changes and efficient cooling, making them suitable for smaller or less thermally demanding applications.</p></p>
