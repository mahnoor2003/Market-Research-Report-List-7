<p><p>The Granular Polysilicon market is segmented by production methods, primarily the Silane Method and the Metallurgical Method. The Silane Method involves the chemical vapor deposition of silane gas to produce high-purity polysilicon, widely used in semiconductor and solar industries. The Metallurgical Method refines metallurgical-grade silicon through physical and chemical processes, yielding lower-purity polysilicon suitable for cost-sensitive applications like solar panels. Both methods cater to different market needs, balancing purity, cost, and application-specific requirements in the growing demand for polysilicon.</p></p>
