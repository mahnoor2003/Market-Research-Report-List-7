<p><p>The paper packaging tape market is primarily divided into two types: adhesive and non-adhesive paper packaging tape. Adhesive paper packaging tape features a sticky backing that securely adheres to surfaces, making it ideal for sealing packages and cartons. In contrast, non-adhesive paper packaging tape relies on pressure or friction for adherence and is often used for bundling or strapping items. Both types offer eco-friendly options, catering to increasing consumer demand for sustainable packaging solutions in various industries.</p></p>
