<p><strong>The Micro Wind Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro wind turbines market comprises two primary types: Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs feature blades that rotate around a horizontal axis, typically offering higher efficiency and performance in areas with consistent wind flow. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, making them suitable for turbulent wind conditions and less dependent on wind direction. Both types cater to small-scale energy needs, supporting distributed renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
