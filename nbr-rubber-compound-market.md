<p><p>The NBR rubber compound market can be categorized into three main types: block type, particles/crumb type, and powder type. Block type consists of solid rubber blocks, ideal for applications requiring durability and strength. Particles/crumb type features small rubber granules that are easily incorporated into various applications, enhancing flexibility and performance. Powder type, consisting of fine rubber particles, is utilized in coatings, adhesives, and sealants for its excellent dispersion properties, enabling uniform application and improved bonding.</p></p>
