<p><p>The pediatric medicines market encompasses various administration routes, including enteral, parenteral, and topical medications. Enteral medicines are delivered directly into the gastrointestinal tract, often in the form of oral solutions or suspensions, providing ease of use and effectiveness. Parenteral medications are administered through injections, offering rapid therapeutic effects for critical conditions. Topical medicines are applied directly to the skin or mucous membranes, allowing localized treatment with fewer systemic side effects. Each type addresses different therapeutic needs in pediatric care.</p></p>
