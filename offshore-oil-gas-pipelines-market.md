<p><p>The offshore oil and gas pipelines market comprises various types of pipes, primarily Electric Resistance Welded (ERW), Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded (LSAW), and Spiral Submerged Arc Welded (SSAW) pipes. ERW pipes are known for their efficiency in smaller diameter applications, while LSAW pipes are preferred for larger diameters and high-pressure projects due to their superior strength. SSAW pipes feature a spiral weld, making them suitable for a wide range of offshore applications, combining flexibility and durability.</p></p>
