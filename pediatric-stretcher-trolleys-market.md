<p><p>The pediatric stretcher trolleys market is categorized into four main types: manual, hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic. Manual trolleys rely on human effort for adjustments, providing a cost-effective option. Hydraulic trolleys utilize fluid power for smooth height and position adjustments, enhancing ease of use. Electric trolleys incorporate motors for automatic operation, offering convenience and precision. Pneumatic trolleys use air pressure for adjustments, ensuring seamless maneuverability. Each type caters to specific needs in hospitals and emergency services for transporting pediatric patients.</p></p>
