<p><p>The PolyJet 3D printers market is categorized into two primary types: wax and polymer resin. Wax-based printers are typically used for creating models and designs that require high detail and accuracy, often utilized in investment casting. In contrast, polymer resin printers utilize photopolymer materials to produce intricate prototypes with smooth finishes, commonly employed in industries like jewelry and dental applications. Both types leverage the same jetting technology, enabling rapid prototyping and versatile production capabilities.</p></p>
