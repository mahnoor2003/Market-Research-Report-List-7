<p><p>The emergency generators market is primarily divided into portable generators and standby generators. Portable generators are designed for temporary use, easily transportable to provide power during outages or outdoor activities, making them ideal for residential and recreational purposes. In contrast, standby generators are permanently installed systems that automatically activate during power failures, offering a reliable power source for homes and businesses. They typically run on natural gas or propane, ensuring seamless continuity of power without user intervention during emergencies.</p></p>
