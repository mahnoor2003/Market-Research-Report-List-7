<p><strong>The Heat Sinks for Electronic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Heat Sinks</li><li>Passive Heat Sinks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat sinks are crucial components in electronic equipment, designed to dissipate heat generated by components. Active heat sinks utilize fans or other mechanical systems to enhance airflow and improve heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, passive heat sinks rely solely on thermal conduction and natural convection, using materials with high thermal conductivity to transfer heat away from components without additional power sources. Both types play essential roles in maintaining optimal operating temperatures and ensuring equipment longevity.</p></p>
