<p><ul><li>Dual Common Anode</li><li>Dual Common Cathode</li><li>Single</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrafast rectifiers market features three main types: Dual Common Anode, Dual Common Cathode, and Single rectifiers. Dual Common Anode rectifiers have two cathodes connected to a common anode, allowing for efficient current handling in applications like power supplies. Dual Common Cathode rectifiers, conversely, have a shared cathode, making them suitable for similar high-speed applications. Single rectifiers consist of one anode and one cathode, ideal for simpler circuits. Each type caters to specific needs in high-frequency and high-efficiency power conversion.</p></p>
