<p><p>The debris loaders market is categorized into two primary types: skid mount debris loaders and hitch mount debris loaders. In contrast, hitch mount debris loaders are portable and can easily attach to various vehicles, providing flexibility for different job sites. Each type serves specific needs in landscape maintenance, municipal cleaning, and construction debris removal, making them essential for effective debris management.</p></p>
