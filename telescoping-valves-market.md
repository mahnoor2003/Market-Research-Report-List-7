<p><strong>The Telescoping Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rising Telescoping Valves</li><li>Non-Rising Telescoping Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telescoping valves market is primarily divided into two types: rising telescoping valves and non-rising telescoping valves. Rising telescoping valves feature a shaft that extends or contracts as the valve opens or closes, ensuring visibility of the valve position and facilitating operation in various applications. Non-rising telescoping valves, on the other hand, have a compact design as their shafts remain within the valve body during operation, making them suitable for installations with space constraints. Both types are essential in fluid control across industries.</p></p>
