PAMAM dendrimers, with their highly branched, tree-like architecture, offer versatile applications in drug delivery, gene therapy, and diagnostics due to their customizable surface functionalities. Both types are integral to advancements in nanomedicine, enabling precise and controlled release of therapeutic agents, enhancing treatment efficacy while minimizing side effects.</p></p>
