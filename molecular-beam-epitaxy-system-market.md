<p><ul><li>Normal MBE</li><li>Laser MBE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) system market is primarily categorized into Normal MBE and Laser MBE types. Normal MBE involves the deposition of materials through thermal beams in a high-vacuum environment, ideal for producing high-purity thin films and semiconductor structures. In contrast, Laser MBE uses laser-driven heating to create molecular beams, allowing for enhanced control over material properties and deposition rates. These systems cater to diverse applications, including electronics, optoelectronics, and materials science, driving innovation in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
