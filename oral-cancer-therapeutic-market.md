<p><p>The oral cancer therapeutic market encompasses various treatment modalities, primarily targeting therapy and chemotherapy. Targeted therapy focuses on specific cancer cell characteristics, utilizing drugs that disrupt specific pathways or proteins involved in tumor growth, thereby minimizing damage to healthy cells. In contrast, chemotherapy employs cytotoxic agents that kill rapidly dividing cells, impacting both cancerous and normal tissues, leading to a broader side effect profile. Together, these approaches aim to effectively manage oral cancer while improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
