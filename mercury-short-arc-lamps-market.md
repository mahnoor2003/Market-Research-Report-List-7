<p><p>The Mercury Short Arc Lamps market is categorized into two main types: DC Mercury Short Arc Lamps and AC Mercury Short Arc Lamps. In contrast, AC Mercury Short Arc Lamps utilize alternating current, offering higher efficiency and a broader spectrum of light, commonly used in larger-scale applications like stadium lighting and large projection systems.</p></p>
