<p><p>The Atomic Fluorescence Spectrometer (AFS) is an analytical tool used for determining trace levels of various elements in samples through the principle of fluorescence. AFS offers high sensitivity and specificity for elements like mercury, lead, and arsenic, making it an essential instrument in areas such as environmental monitoring, food safety, and clinical analysis.</p><p>The AFS market is experiencing robust growth due to increasing demand for precise analytical instruments in laboratories and regulatory frameworks emphasizing safety and compliance.
